Hi, I am a PostDoc a the University of Bologna. I am working in the ERC project MAGCOW with Dr. Franco Vazza. I wrote my PhD-Thesis at the University of Hamburg under the supervision of Dr. Franco Vazza and Prof. Marcus Brüggen. I studied physics at the Ruhr-Universität Bochum, where I obtained my Bachelor and Master degree, working on Discontinuous Galerkin Methods and Adapative Mesh Refinement in the group of Prof. Rainer Grauer. My research interests are the non-thermal components of galaxy clusters (namely magnetic fields, cosmic-rays and turbulence). For my research, I am using large cosmological simulations. Furthermore, I am interested in numerical methods and high performance computing.
